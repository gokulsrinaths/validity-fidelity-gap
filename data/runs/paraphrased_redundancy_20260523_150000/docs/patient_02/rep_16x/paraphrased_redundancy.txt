Encounter Note (ED)

Patient: Priya Shah
Medical Record Number: 002-11290
Date of Birth: November 3, 1990

Encounter Date: March 18, 2026

Chief Complaint:
The patient presented with right lower quadrant abdominal pain.

History of Present Illness:
A 35-year-old female patient reported experiencing 12 hours of right lower quadrant pain accompanied by nausea and low-grade fever.

Assessment:
The patient was diagnosed with acute appendicitis.

Procedures:
On March 18, 2026, a laparoscopic appendectomy was performed.

Medications:
Pre-operatively, the patient received ceftriaxone 1 g IV and metronidazole 500 mg IV. Post-operatively, acetaminophen 650 mg PO was prescribed every 6 hours as needed for pain.

Observations:
The patient's temperature was 38.1 C and white blood cell count was 14.2 K/uL.

Plan:
Post-operative care will be provided, and the patient will be advanced to a regular diet as tolerated. A follow-up appointment in the surgery clinic is scheduled for two weeks.
